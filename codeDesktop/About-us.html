About Us

We are dedicated to rescuing and rehoming abandoned, stray, and surrendered pets. Our shelter provides a safe and loving environment for animals in need while we work tirelessly to find them permanent homes. Since our founding in 2024, we have helped hundreds of pets find their forever families, and we continue to be a trusted partner in our community for promoting responsible pet ownership.
Our mission at Adopt a Pet is to save the lives of homeless pets and promote responsible pet ownership within our community. We are dedicated to providing the highest level of care to animals in our shelter, while actively working to find them loving homes. Through education, outreach, and partnership with local organizations, we aim to reduce pet overpopulation, prevent animal neglect, and create a future where every pet has a safe and caring home. Together, with the support of our community, we believe we can make a lasting impact on the lives of pets and their families.
